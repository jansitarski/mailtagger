To access the mailtagger UI:

1. Port-forward to the service:
   kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "mailtagger.fullname" . }} -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} 8080:{{ .Values.service.port }}

2. Get the setup token from the logs:
   kubectl logs -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} deploy/{{ include "mailtagger.fullname" . }} | grep "Setup Token"

3. Open in your browser:
   http://localhost:8080/setup?token=YOUR_TOKEN
